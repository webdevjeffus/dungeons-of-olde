Challenge Value Description
Trivial 4

Ordinary, non-heroic people can do it most of the time.

Dribble down the court without anyone guarding you.
Easy 6

Heroes and professionals can do it most of the time. Ordinary folks can do it from time to time.

Hit a free-throw in practice.
Hard 8

Heroes can often do it, if it uses one of their better stats. For ordinary people, success at Hard Challenges is a once-a-year occurrence.

Make a basket in a college game.
Heroic 10

A hero can sometimes do it, if it’s based on his best stat and he has a little skill or luck. Succeeding at a Heroic Challenge is a once-in-a-lifetime event for ordinary people.

Hit a three-pointer in an NBA game.
Superhuman 12

A hero might pull it off, if it’s based on his best stat, and he has both skill and luck.

Nail a buzzer-beater from half court to win an important game.
Impossible 15

A hero might do it once in a lifetime, if everything goes his way,
and the relevant gods are smiling on him.

Put up 8 points in the last 9 seconds to win in the playoffs.
(Google “Reggie Miller 8 points in 9 seconds”).
